Junior Software Developer

Yakup (Jacob) Bilir loves programming, video editing, learning new languages, travelling, hot yoga, running and lifting heavy things. He recently graduated from Red Deer Polytechnic University in AB, and He does not love talking about himself in the 3rd person. Previously, he spent almost a decade sailing on cruise ships around the world building life experience as an acting shore excursion manager, precisely he visited 118 countries and 6 continents.
He has good knowledge of Troubleshooting, IT tools and services, Javascript, HTML5, CSS3, MySQL and Python. Also built a 3D Game with Unity. He is looking for an opportunity to expand his skills in Software Development. Yakup values building positive relationships with customers and coworkers. He loves keeping himself up to date on field, industry and technology trends in terms of infrastructure and products that serve hundreds of millions of users while working as an IT Specialist at Apple.
Check out his resume, GitHub and LinkedIn for more info.
